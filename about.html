About Asom Natya Sanmilan, Jorhat

Our team is Asom Natya Sanmilan, Jorhat Our group belongs to the Soumar Peeth zone. Respecting the motto of the mother organization, we are also carrying on a responsible amateur movement. The team constitutes of almost 50 members and an honorable advisor panel. Some of the dramas we have already staged are – ‘Bhobisyotor Buronji’, Yusuf – Zuleikha’ , ‘Vivah: Eti Bahir Sur’ ‘Brahmagupta’, ‘Jagatguru.com’ and ‘Dapon’ etc.’ Bhobisyotor Buronji’ received seven state level awards. Our most successful play ‘Yusuf- Zuleikha’ delighted immensely the audience of 10th Poorvottar Natya Samaroh at Sivasagar Natya Mandir, held by NSD, Delhi. The play also snatched eight state level awards till now. Another extraordinary play ‘Vivah: Eti Bahir Sur’ has been highly appreciated throughout Assam. This play also selected for 11th Poorvottar Natya Samaroh at Dimapur, held by NSD, Delhi. The play was awarded the BEST TEAM for twice and was also honoured with the SECOND and THIRD BEST TEAM for once in State Level Drama Competitions. Apart from these full length plays, the team also concentrates in one act plays, solo performances etc. Our team members namely Pankaj Kalita has achieved the BEST and Swagata Bharali has achieved the SECOND BEST solo act performer award in 7th All India Dance Drama and Folk Song competition . Our team organised a grand children workshop this summer-- from 4th July to 27th July, 2018 with around 80 participants in which the trainees boldly demonstrated three plays written by Mr. Tapos Tarun Saikia titled—‘KHEL’(Direction- Gauranga Kishor Borah), ‘LUVOR TEKELI, GHAMOR TEKELI’ (Direction- Pankaj Kalita) and ‘KOTHA KUWA GHOR’(Direction- Swagata Bharali) along with two dance-dramas. We are proud to announce that our team member Swagata Bharali got the chance to complete her theatre training from NSD, SIKKIM and Soumarjyoti Borah got chance to complete his training from NSD, Tripura. We used to run many in-house programmes to develop new methodology to boost the amateur movement, rectify the problems related to amateur theatre and to sort them out. We hope and believe that amateur theatre will help to reform the society, by offering solution to the problems of mankind.
